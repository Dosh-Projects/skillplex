Our Training

Our range of training courses spans major sectors. We have alliances and partnerships with various institutions and certification bodies.

Project Management Courses

These courses provide participants with project management skills and prepare them for industry-recognized certifications.

  • PMI - Project Management Professional Exam Prep Course

    • Prepares participants for the PMP exam with project management fundamentals, lifecycle, risk management, and more. Duration: 4 days.

  • Risk Management Professional (PMI-RMP) Prep Course

    • Teaches risk identification, analysis, and response techniques aligned with the PMI-RMP certification. Duration: 3 days.

  • Construction Project Management

    • Focuses on planning, execution, and control for successful construction project delivery. Duration: 3 days.

  • PMI-Certified Business Analysis Course

    • Trains participants in business analysis fundamentals and prepares them for PMI-PBA certification. Duration: 4 days.

  • Primavera P6 Basic Training

    • Introduces project creation, activity management, and dashboards in Primavera P6. Duration: 3 days.

  • Primavera P6 Advanced Course

    • Explores advanced project management in Primavera P6, including resource allocation and earned value analysis. Duration: 3 days.

  • Primavera Risk Analysis Course

    • Focuses on applying Monte Carlo risk analysis to scheduling tools. Duration: 3 days.

  • PMI-Agile Certified Professional (PMI-ACP)

    • Provides expertise in agile methodologies like Scrum, Kanban, and Lean. Duration: 4 days.

American Society for Non-Destructive Testing (ASNT)

Courses designed for professionals working with inspection and testing in manufacturing and in-service industries.

  • Magnetic Particle Inspection

    • Teaches principles of magnetism, methods of testing, and sensitivity assessment. Duration: 2 days.

  • Radiographic Testing

    • Focuses on X- and gamma-ray properties, film processing, and defect analysis. Duration: 8 days.

  • Ultrasonic Testing

    • Includes sound detection, weld flaw inspection, and practical exercises. Duration: 8 days.

  • Visual Testing

    • Covers visual inspection fundamentals, equipment use, and practical applications. Duration: 2 days.

Corrosion Control Courses

These courses focus on surface treatment and application of protective coatings.

  • Train the Painter Course

    • Provides comprehensive training in blasting and painting techniques. Duration: 8 days.

American Petroleum Institute (API) – Inspection Courses

Inspection courses aimed at maintaining safety, performance, and compliance in the oil and gas industry.

  • API 510 - Pressure Vessel Inspector Preparatory Course

    • Covers pressure vessel inspection and maintenance based on API 510 standards. Duration: 6 days.

  • API 570 - Piping Inspector Preparatory Course

    • Focuses on the inspection, repair, and maintenance of piping systems. Duration: 6 days.

  • API 653 - Above Ground Storage Tank Inspector Course

    • Focuses on inspection and maintenance of aboveground storage tanks. Duration: 6 days.

  • API 580 - Risk Based Inspection Preparatory Course

    • Teaches RBI principles and preparation for API 580 certification. Duration: 6 days.

Cost Engineering Courses (AACE)

Courses designed to enhance skills in cost estimation, planning, and control.

  • Certified Cost Professional (CCP) Preparatory Course

    • Teaches cost estimation and control techniques aligned with global standards. Duration: 5 days.

  • Certified Estimating Professional (CEP) Preparatory Course

    • Prepares participants for AACE-CEP certification with advanced estimation techniques. Duration: 5 days.

Human Resource Certification Institute (HRCI) Courses

Professional HR certifications designed to enhance global HR knowledge and skills.

  • Professional in Human Resources-International (PHRi)

    • Focuses on technical and operational HR principles. Duration: 4 days.

  • Senior Professional in Human Resources-International (SPHRi)

    • Covers advanced HR strategies, policies, and leadership techniques. Duration: 4 days.

Lean Six Sigma Certification (IASSC)

Six Sigma courses that focus on quality improvement methodologies and certification preparation.

  • Lean Six Sigma Green Belt Certification

    • Provides practical training in Six Sigma DMAIC methodology for team leaders. Duration: 3 days.

  • Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Certification

    • Prepares participants for leadership roles in quality improvement projects. Duration: 5 days.
